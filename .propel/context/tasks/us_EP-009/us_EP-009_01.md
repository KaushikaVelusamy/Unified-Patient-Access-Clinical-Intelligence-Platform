# User Story: PDF Appointment Confirmation

## Epic: EP-009

### As a patient,
I want to receive my appointment details as a PDF via email after booking,
so that I have a clear and accessible record of my appointment.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- The system generates a PDF with appointment details after booking.
- The PDF is sent as an email attachment to the patient.
- Patients can access and download the PDF from their email.
- If email delivery fails, the system retries and/or notifies support.

#### Business Value
Improves communication, reduces confusion.

#### Effort Estimation
5 Story Points
