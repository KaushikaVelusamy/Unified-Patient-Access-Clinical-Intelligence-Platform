# User Story: Insurance Pre-check

## Epic: EP-005

### As a patient,
I want the system to perform an insurance pre-check against internal records before my appointment,
so that I can be informed of my coverage and avoid claim denials.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- The system allows patients to enter insurance details during booking/intake.
- Insurance pre-check is performed against internal dummy records.
- Patients are notified of pre-check results before appointment confirmation.
- Staff can view insurance status for each patient.

#### Business Value
Reduces claim denials, improves patient transparency.

#### Effort Estimation
5 Story Points
