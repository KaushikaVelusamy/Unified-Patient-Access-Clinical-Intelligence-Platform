# User Story: No Show Risk Assessment & Handling

## Epic: EP-007

### As a staff member,
I want the system to assess no-show risk and allow me to mark no-shows,
so that I can proactively intervene and reduce lost revenue.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- The system evaluates appointments for no-show risk using rule-based logic.
- High-risk appointments are flagged and staff are notified.
- Staff can mark appointments as "No Show".
- All no-show actions are logged immutably for audit and future risk assessment.
- Staff can review no-show history for each patient.

#### Business Value
Reduces lost revenue, enables proactive intervention.

#### Effort Estimation
8 Story Points
