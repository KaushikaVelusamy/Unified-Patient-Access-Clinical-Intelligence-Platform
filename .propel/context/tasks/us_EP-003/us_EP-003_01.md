# User Story: Clinical Document Upload & Data Extraction

## Epic: EP-003

### As a patient,
I want to upload my clinical documents and have the system extract relevant data,
so that my medical profile is accurate and up-to-date for my care team.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- Patients can upload clinical documents in supported formats.
- The system extracts structured data from uploaded documents.
- Extracted data is reviewed and confirmed by staff.
- The system highlights any data conflicts for staff resolution.
- Unified patient profile is updated with new data.

#### Business Value
Saves staff time, improves data accuracy, supports clinical decision-making.

#### Effort Estimation
8 Story Points
