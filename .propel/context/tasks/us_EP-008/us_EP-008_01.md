# User Story: Automated Reminders & Notifications

## Epic: EP-008

### As a patient,
I want to receive automated reminders and notifications for my appointments,
so that I am reminded in advance and can better manage my schedule.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- The system sends reminders via SMS/Email at configurable intervals.
- Patients can opt in/out of reminders and notifications.
- Calendar sync is supported for Google/Outlook.
- Staff can view reminder delivery status and notification logs.

#### Business Value
Reduces no-shows, improves patient engagement.

#### Effort Estimation
5 Story Points
