# User Story: Patient Dashboard

## Epic: EP-010

### As a patient,
I want a secure dashboard to manage my appointments, documents, intake, and notifications,
so that I can easily access and control all aspects of my healthcare journey.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- Patients can log in and access a personalized dashboard.
- Dashboard displays upcoming and past appointments, uploaded documents, and notifications.
- Patients can upload documents and complete intake forms from the dashboard.
- Notifications and reminders are visible and actionable.
- All actions are secure and HIPAA-compliant.

#### Business Value
Empowers patients, increases engagement.

#### Effort Estimation
8 Story Points
