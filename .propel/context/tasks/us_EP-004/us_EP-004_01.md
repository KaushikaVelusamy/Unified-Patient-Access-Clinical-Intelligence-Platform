# User Story: Medical Coding & Conflict Detection

## Epic: EP-004

### As a staff member,
I want the system to map ICD-10/CPT codes from patient data and highlight medication conflicts,
so that I can ensure accurate billing and improve patient safety.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- The system automatically maps ICD-10 and CPT codes from aggregated clinical data.
- Medication conflicts are detected and highlighted in the patient profile.
- Staff are notified of any detected conflicts.
- Staff can review and resolve conflicts.
- All actions are logged for audit.

#### Business Value
Ensures accurate billing, improves patient safety.

#### Effort Estimation
8 Story Points
