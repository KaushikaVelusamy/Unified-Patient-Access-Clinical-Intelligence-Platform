# User Story: Staff Walk-in & Queue Management

## Epic: EP-006

### As a staff member,
I want to manage walk-ins, same-day queues, and mark arrivals,
so that I can streamline clinic operations and reduce patient wait times.

#### Acceptance Criteria
- Staff can add walk-in patients and manage same-day queues.
- The system tracks patient arrivals and updates queue status in real time.
- Staff can mark patients as "Arrived" or "No Show".
- The system notifies staff of queue changes and patient arrivals.
- All queue actions are logged for audit.

#### Business Value
Streamlines clinic operations, reduces wait times.

#### Effort Estimation
8 Story Points
